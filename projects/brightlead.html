The brief

A UK lead generation agency needed a high-converting landing page for their new B2B service. The page needed to capture qualified leads, integrate with HubSpot, and load in under 2 seconds on mobile.

What I built

  • Custom landing page with mobile-first responsive design
  • HubSpot CRM integration via API (form submissions → contacts)
  • A/B testing setup with Google Optimize (3 variants)
  • Performance optimised — 98 Lighthouse score
  • Animated micro-interactions for engagement
  • GDPR-compliant cookie consent and form checkboxes

Tech stack

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, HubSpot API, Google Optimize, Netlify

Result

32% conversion rate (industry average: 12%). 450+ leads captured in the first month. Page loads in 0.8s on 4G.
