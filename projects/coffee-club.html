The Coffee Club

Full WooCommerce store with subscription model

WooCommerce8 weeksWhite-label
TC

The brief

A premium coffee roastery based in Yorkshire needed a complete e-commerce solution. They wanted to sell individual products, offer subscription boxes, and manage wholesale orders for cafés — all from one platform.

What I built

  • Custom WooCommerce theme with unique coffee-shop aesthetic
  • Subscription engine for weekly/monthly coffee deliveries
  • Stripe payment gateway with saved cards
  • Wholesale pricing tier with minimum order quantities
  • Stock management with low-stock email alerts
  • Multi-currency support (£, €, $)

Tech stack

WooCommerce, Custom Theme, Stripe, PHP, SCSS, Mailchimp integration

Result

£12k in revenue in the first month. 85 active subscribers. Average order value of £32. Site loads in under 1s with full page caching.
