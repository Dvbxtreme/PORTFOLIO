Corporate website for a UK surveying company

WordPress6 weeksWhite-label
SV

The brief

A UK-based property surveying company needed a professional website to replace their outdated 5-page site. The new site needed to establish trust with homeowners and estate agents, showcase their services clearly, and generate qualified leads through contact form submissions.

What I built

  • 12-page WordPress site with custom Gutenberg blocks
  • Online booking calendar integrated with Calendly
  • Service pages with detailed SEO-optimised content
  • Interactive property search by postcode
  • Client portal for report downloads (password protected)
  • Google Maps integration showing coverage area

Tech stack

WordPress, ACF Pro, Custom Post Types, PHP, MySQL, SCSS, Leaflet.js

Result

10× increase in contact form submissions within the first 3 months. Page load time under 1.2s. Keyword rankings on page 1 for "surveyor [city]" across 6 locations.
